Projects

Lasca


Emanuel Lasker's game done with JS


Lasca is a board game similar to checkers but with an important difference, which is that the captured enemy piece is not taken out of the game but remains on the board and can be released. Although the rules are simple, during the game, very complex positions are presented, which gives it great strategic wealth.

MAL3T


MAchine Learning Tic-Tac-Toe


MAL3T is a personal project pointing towards acquiring knowledge in this specific field and other programming languages. It consists in playing tic-tac-toe against a self-improving AI that stores the moves it makes (wether it leads to win or lose) to choose the best decision.
