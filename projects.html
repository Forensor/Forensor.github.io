ABOUT PROJECTS GITHUB SOCIAL RESUME

I love to tinker with a lot of programming languages... Here are some of my personal projects:


purescript-amazons

A viewless library to control/validate matches of The Game of the Amazons. Pure and free from side effects. From the beginning I intended to increase the number of individuals who appreciate the beauty of this magnificent game.


jinsei

With our day-to-day worries and forgetful minds it can sometimes be difficult to keep our weight at healthy levels. Jinsei is an app that will help you stay fit by monitoring and predicting your weight based on your stats from the last 20 days.


brainhask

Brainhask is a tiny interpreter for the Brainf**k language. It features an inline/file input interpreter, and a REPL.
